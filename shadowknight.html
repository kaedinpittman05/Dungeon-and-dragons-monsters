Shadow Knight

  • Armor Class 20(natural armor)
  • Hit Points 410 (20d10+ 100)
  • Speed 30ft.

Stats

  • Str 24(+7)
  • Dex 14 (+2)
  • Con 20(+5)
  • Int 14(+2)
  • wis 11(+0)
  • Cha 16(+3)

Damage Immunities

  • fire
  • posion
  • psychic

Condition Immunities

  • Charmed
  • exhaustion
  • frightend
  • paralyzed
  • petrified
  • poisoned

Senses

  • darkvision 120ft
  • passive Perception 10

Challenge 16(15,000 XP

Abilities

Fire Deflect: Whenever the Shadow Knight is subjected to fire damage, it transfers the damage to a target creature or object within it 100ft of it.

Immutable Form: The Shadow Knight is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.

Magic Restitance: The Shadow Knight has advantage on saving throws aginst spells and other magical effects.

Magic Weapons: The Shadow Knights Weapon attacks are magical.

Actions

  • Multiattack: The shadow Knight makes two melee attacks
  • Sword: Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 10ft, hit 23(3d10+7) slashing damage.
  • Shadow Move: The Shadow Knight can use its move action to teleport to a shadow within 200ft
